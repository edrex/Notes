I decide the attempt idea capture.

Journaling at 4am with the lights off

I don’t want to open a laptop, becase THE SEA will rush in, but I don’t have any other mode of capture I am habituated to (ie, paper), so I decide to risk it. I almost turn the lights on, but decide against it.Sensory deprevation is great for writing (if you are constantly overstimulated by the world, like me).

Augghh the screen, it burns my eyes, and the sea of icons, text, and media already present threaten to sear my brain!), so I act quickly: - with eyes averted I - Switch to terminal workspace - full screen terminal window - dim the screen: sudo echo 130 > /sys/class/backlight/intel_backlight/brightness - Type journal - Zoom waaaay in - set keyboard brightness to zero - cover the laptop charging light with the lip of my laptop stand
