For hardware testing, use SysRescueCD: http://www.system-rescue-cd.org/

  1. Download the latest image
  2. Write it to your USB stick. There are some GUI tools like Etcher for this but I suggest just doing it from the command line.

Find the device for your USB stick:

lsblk

This will list all your “block” (storage) devices, including disks and partitions.

Disks are named like

{disk type}{disk number}

while partitions are named like {disk}

{disk type}{disk number}p{partition number}

(so just with a pN at the end)
