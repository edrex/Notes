One annoyance I’ve had for awhile is how long it takes to resume from suspend on my XPS 13 laptop when a USBC hub is plugged in. I’ve tested with various combinations of plugged USB devices:

  • USB C hub
    • HDMI
    • Mouse
    • Keyboard
    • Power

Resume time: ~13.5 seconds Just USBC hub: 12334 ms

  • No devices: <3 seconds

Mouse+keyboard plugged directly: <3 seconds

USB A 3.0 hub with mouse+keyboard: Total Resume Time: 2924 ms

Intel’s sleepgraph tool is an amazing facility for tracking down suspend/resume performance issues:
